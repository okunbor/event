Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join an event?

To join an event, simply browse our event listings, select the event that piques your interest, and follow the registration instructions provided. It's that easy!

Are the events free to attend?

Event admission fees vary depending on the event organizers. Some events may offer free admission, while others may require a registration fee. Check the event details for more information.

Can I submit my own event?

Absolutely! If you're organizing an academic or tech event and want to reach a wider audience, you can submit your event details to us. Our team will review the submission and get in touch with you regarding the event listing process.

How do I stay updated on upcoming events?

Stay in the loop by subscribing to our newsletter and following us on social media. We regularly share updates on upcoming events, exclusive event highlights, and insider insights to keep you informed and engaged!

Can I connect with other event attendees?

Yes, you can! Eventify provides networking opportunities for event attendees to connect, interact, and exchange ideas. Engage with like-minded individuals, expand your professional network, and make meaningful connections at our events!
